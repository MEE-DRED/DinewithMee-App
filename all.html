About Us

Dine with Mee is a digital platform dedicated to bringing the rich flavors and cultural heritage of Nigerian cuisine to Nigerians in the diaspora, West Africans, and food enthusiasts around the world. We make it easy to order authentic Nigerian meals, shop for traditional foodstuff and ingredients, and access expert-led telenutrition services — all in one place.

Whether you crave the comfort of home-cooked jollof rice, need specialty spices straight from Nigeria, or want a personalized nutrition plan tailored to your lifestyle, Dine with Mee bridges the gap between tradition, taste, and technology.

Our platform combines culinary excellence with modern convenience, ensuring that every order, consultation, and interaction reflects our passion for quality, authenticity, and customer satisfaction.

Mission

To bridge the gap between food, culture, and health by delivering authentic Nigerian meals, providing access to premium ingredients, and empowering individuals through expert-led nutrition services — all powered by innovative digital solutions.

Vision

To become the leading African food and nutrition platform worldwide, preserving culinary heritage, promoting healthier lifestyles, and connecting communities through the shared love of Nigerian cuisine.
